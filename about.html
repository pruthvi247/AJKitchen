Our Story

From a Small Kitchen to 500+ Events

AJ Caterer started with a simple dream — to serve food that feels like home, even at the grandest celebrations. What began as a small family-run kitchen has grown into one of the most trusted catering services in the region.

Founded with the belief that great food brings people together, we've spent years perfecting our craft. From our first wedding order for 50 guests to now serving 5,000+ guests at corporate campuses daily, every milestone has been fueled by the same passion — cooking with love and serving with pride.
